Translate <rect> with transform in style.

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 10 10">
  <rect x="1" y="1" width="2" height="3" style="transform:translate(1px,2px)"/>
</svg>

@@@

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 10 10">
  <rect x="2" y="3" width="2" height="3"/>
</svg>
