Don't modify class attribute if there are attribute selectors on "class".

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 10 10">
  <style>
    rect[class] {fill:green}
    rect.blue {fill:blue}
  </style>
  <rect width="2" height="3" class="red"/>
</svg>
