Don't remove empty nodes for xml:space="preserve".

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 400 40">
  <text x="10" y="10" xml:space="preserve">
    <tspan>this is a</tspan>
        test    
    <tspan>of lots of spaces around test</tspan>
  </text>
</svg>
