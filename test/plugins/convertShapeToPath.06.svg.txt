Don't convert <rect> with no width/height.

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 15 15">
  <rect y="0" width="2" height="1.5"/>
  <rect y="3" width="0" height="1.5"/>
  <rect y="6" width="2" height="0"/>
  <rect y="9" height="1.5"/>
  <rect y="12" width="2"/>
</svg>

@@@

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 15 15">
    <path d="M0 0H2V1.5H0z"/>
    <rect y="3" width="0" height="1.5"/>
    <rect y="6" width="2" height="0"/>
    <rect y="9" height="1.5"/>
    <rect y="12" width="2"/>
</svg>
