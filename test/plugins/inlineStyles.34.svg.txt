Parse style properties when inlining.

===

<svg viewBox="0 0 20 20"
  xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg">
  <style>.a{stroke:red;stroke-width:0.7}</style>
  <rect width="4" height="5" class="a"/>
</svg>

@@@

<svg viewBox="0 0 20 20" xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg">
  <rect width="4" height="5" style="stroke:red;stroke-width:.7"/>
</svg>
