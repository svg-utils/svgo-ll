Don't convert style to attribute if transform attribute is present.

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 30 30">
  <rect y="3" width="3" height="2" transform="rotate(15)" style="fill:red"/>
</svg>

@@@

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 30 30">
  <rect y="3" width="3" height="2" transform="rotate(15)" style="fill:red"/>
</svg>
