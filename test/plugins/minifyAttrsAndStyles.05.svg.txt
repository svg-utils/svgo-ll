Allow conversion of styles to attributes if there is a transform attribute.

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 30 40">
  <rect width="3" height="3" y="5" transform="rotate(15)" style="fill:red"/>
</svg>

@@@

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 30 40">
  <rect width="3" height="3" y="5" transform="rotate(15)" fill="red"/>
</svg>
