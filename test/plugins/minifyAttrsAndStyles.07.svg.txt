Convert attribute to style if it makes things smaller or the same.

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 30 30">
  <rect y="3" width="3" height="2" fill="red" stroke="green" stroke-width="2" opacity=".1"/>
</svg>

@@@

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 30 30">
  <rect y="3" width="3" height="2" style="fill:red;stroke:green;stroke-width:2;opacity:.1"/>
</svg>
