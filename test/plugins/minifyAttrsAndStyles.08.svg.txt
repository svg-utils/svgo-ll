font-size and letter-spacing can be unitless as attributes

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 30 40">
  <text y="10" font-size="8" letter-spacing="10">XX</text>
  <text y="20" style="font-size:8;letter-spacing:10">XX</text>
</svg>
