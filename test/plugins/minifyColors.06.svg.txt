Convert "color" attribute values.

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" style="color:#888888" viewBox="0 0 100 100">
  <rect x="10" y="10" width="10" height="10" fill="currentColor"/>
  <rect x="10" y="30" width="10" height="10" style="fill:currentColor"/>
  <rect x="10" y="50" width="10" height="10" style="fill:inherit"/>
  <rect x="10" y="70" width="10" height="10"/>
</svg>

@@@

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" style="color:#888" viewBox="0 0 100 100">
