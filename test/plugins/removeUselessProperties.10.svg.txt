Don't remove identity transform that is overriding style.

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 10 10">
  <style>rect{transform:rotate(25deg)}</style>
  <rect y="5" width="2" height="3" style="transform:translate(0,0)"/>
</svg>

@@@
