Convert styles to classes.

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 30 30">
  <rect y="3" width="3" height="2" style="fill:red"/>
  <rect y="7" width="3" height="2" style="fill:red"/>
</svg>

@@@

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 30 30">
  <style>.a{fill:red}</style>
  <rect class="a" y="3" width="3" height="2"/>
  <rect class="a" y="7" width="3" height="2"/>
</svg>
