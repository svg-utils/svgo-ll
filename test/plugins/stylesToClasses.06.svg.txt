When creating new class names, don't collide with existing classes.

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 30 30">
  <style>.b{color:blue}</style>
  <rect y="3" fill="cornflowerblue" width="3" height="2"/>
  <rect y="7" fill="cornflowerblue" width="3" height="2"/>
  <rect y="11" fill="cornflowerblue" width="3" height="2"/>
  <rect y="15" fill="cornflowerblue" width="3" height="2"/>
  <rect y="19" class="a" width="3" height="2"/>
</svg>

@@@

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 30 30">
