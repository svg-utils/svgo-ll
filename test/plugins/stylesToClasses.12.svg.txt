Don't convert to classes if there is anything other than class selectors in existing <style>s.

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" viewBox="0 0 50 50">
  <style>#a{fill:green}</style>
  <rect x="2" y="2" width="10" height="10" style="fill:red"/>
  <rect x="14" y="2" width="10" height="10" style="fill:red"/>
  <rect x="26" y="2" width="10" height="10" style="fill:red" id="a"/>
</svg>

@@@
